Potato Bacon Soup

  • 3 Potatoes peeled and diced

  • 1/2 onion

  • 1 diced carrot

  • 2 stalks celery diced

  • 1 garlic clove

  • 1/2 package of bacon fried and cut up

  • 2 cans of mushroom soup

  • 2 cups of milk

  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Take half a pack of bacon cut up into small chunks and fry till preferred readiness. Drain fat and dab off excess fat with paper towel. Put into the crockpot.

  2. Next step you are going to cut up half an onion, 1 carrot, 2 stalks of celery, and garlic clove
